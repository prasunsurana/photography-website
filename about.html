hi! my name is prasun surana, and i'm an amateur photographer based in singapore.

photography was my first love as a kid, ever since google earth added the 'earth from above' pictures by yann arthus bertrand to the map. i finally got around to getting my own camera 6 years ago, and while i'm still using the same camera and kit lens, i've had a great time trying to capture unnoticed moments in everyday life.
